Access to programs and services without substantial travel What are the current locations of your students? How can we ensure they have access to our programs and services without substantial travel? Evaluate impact on clinical placements, internships, or other experiential learning components. 4 - Builds on location information (2-3)
Number and types of credits to accept Can you provide a detailed list of the courses and credits your students have completed or are currently enrolled in? Scrutinize any discrepancies in credit hour definitions or course equivalencies. 5 - Depends on program information (1)
Experience, resources, and support services What specific support services are your students currently receiving that we need to match or exceed? Assess adequacy of library resources, technology infrastructure, and specialized academic support services. 6 - Informed by programs (1) and locations (2-4)
Resources to remain stable and meet obligations What are the specific obligations to students that we need to be prepared to meet? Evaluate financial projections and contingency plans for potential enrollment fluctuations. 7 - Builds on programs (1), locations (2-4), and support services (6)
Comprehensive description of arrangements What are the current tuition and fees for your programs? Are there any specific financial arrangements or scholarships we need to be aware of? Scrutinize any changes in total cost of attendance and impact on financial aid eligibility. 8 - Integrates information from 1-7
Financial and Compliance
Financial considerations Are there any specific financial arrangements or considerations we need to discuss? Analyze potential hidden costs and long-term financial implications for students. 9 - Builds on comprehensive arrangements (8)
Consistency with MSCHE standards and federal compliance Can you provide us with the specific MSCHE standards, requirements, policies, and federal compliance regulations we need to adhere to? Assess potential changes in institutional effectiveness metrics and student achievement indicators. 10 - Overarching consideration, informed by all previous sequences
Regulatory compliance Are there any specific state or federal regulations, beyond MSCHE standards, that we need to be aware of and comply with? Evaluate implications for specialized program accreditations and licensure requirements. 11 - Builds on MSCHE standards (10), may require additional research
Finalization
Student records transfer What is the plan for transferring and maintaining student records? How will we ensure continuity of access to these records? Assess data migration processes and long-term accessibility of historical student records. 12 - Can be addressed after most other details are finalized (1-11)
Communication plan How do you plan to communicate this change to your students? How can we collaborate on this communication? Evaluate mechanisms for ongoing communication and addressing student grievances during the transition. 13 - Depends on having a clear understanding of all arrangements (1-12)
Signed teach-out agreement Can you provide a draft of the teach-out agreement for our review and signature? Scrutinize legal language for potential loopholes or ambiguities that could impact student protections. 14 - Final step, incorporates all previous information (1-13)
